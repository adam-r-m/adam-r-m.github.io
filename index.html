Adam Russell Murray

Bio

I'm an Associate Professor in the Department of Philosophy at the University of Manitoba. I work on topics at the intersection of logic, metaphysics, and philosophy of language. Before starting at the U of M I was a lecturer in the Department of Philosophy at the University of Toronto, where I also did my PhD.

In 2025 I'll be the acting Graduate Chair in Philosophy at the U of M. If you're a student looking for information about our graduate program, feel free to drop me a line.
